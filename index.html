About

Hi, I'm David Ramroop, an aspiring Cloud Engineer. I am currently learning about cloud computing and working on projects to build my skills. I am also studying for certifications to validate my knowledge and experience. I am excited to learn more about cloud computing and to work on projects that will help me grow as a Cloud Engineer.

I have over 8 years of experience in the ICT industry with a strong background in software, hardware, and networking. Throughout my career, I have configured, administered, and provided technical support for various network infrastructures and databases. I have worked with both local and virtual network environments and I acquired skills in Active Directory, Azure AD, DNS servers, file servers, Hyper-V, and Linux-based platforms. I also have technical expertise in Office 365 administration, having managed a forest for three years and successfully integrated Azure AD for credential synchronization between cloud and on-prem resources. Additionally, I have experience maintaining 24/7 critical systems, providing customer support, and ensuring seamless ICT operations. Over the last two years, I have been pursuing a degree in Computer Application and Cloud Computing, which has deepened my passion for cloud infrastructure. I have been actively exploring Amazon Web Services and working on various cloud projects to gain hands-on experience. One of my main goals is to document and share step-by-step procedures from my projects while tackling real-world technical challenges. As I progress, my goal is to actively contribute to the field of Cloud Engineering, leveraging my technical expertise to empower business innovation and growth. I am excited about the opportunities that cloud computing offers and look forward to collaborating, learning and making an impact in the industry.
